About Us

The Rupa Foundation is a faith-driven charitable organization dedicated to helping deprived young people in India. We believe every child deserves access to education, health, and hope. Through Christian Aid and local partnerships, we support communities in need with practical help and compassion.

Our Causes

Education Support

Providing school supplies, tuition assistance, and safe learning environments for children in rural areas.

Health & Nutrition

Delivering food programs, medical check-ups, and clean water initiatives to help children thrive.

Faith & Hope

Partnering with local Christian communities to offer mentorship, values, and hope for a better future.
